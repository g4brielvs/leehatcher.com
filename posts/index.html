    A Transgender Man: How My Development Through Adolescence Was Affected

    May 6, 2021 in transgender
    Growing up, I faced a lot of adversity within what Bronfenbrenner would call the microsystem of my ecological system. I was raised in a white lower-middle-class family that encountered a heavy tragedy when I was only eight years old; because of this, my parents struggled with their own mental and emotional well-being, which in turn greatly impacted my life and still does to this day. There were other complexities in my life before then, such as my struggle with my gender identity, that played a large role in my becoming the person I am today.
